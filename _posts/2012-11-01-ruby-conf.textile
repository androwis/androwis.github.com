ruby conf was the first conference I went to where I felt adequately unprepared for. having only been introduced to the world of ruby only a few weeks prior,I was a complete newcomer.

What blew me away was the community. it was intimate conference, but one that was super international. first time I experienced that. there the bad assets were genuinely interested in meeting everyone,

design was taken seriously

morals were most importantly upheld

teaching was the goal
